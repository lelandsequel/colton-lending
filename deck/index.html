01 — Traditional SEO

Search Engine Optimization

The foundation. Making sure Google understands what you do, trusts your site, and ranks you for the right searches.

Technical SEO

Fast load times, mobile-friendly, proper HTML structure, meta tags, schema markup.

On-Page SEO

Keyword-optimized titles, descriptions, headers. Content that matches search intent.

Trust Signals

Schema.org markup tells Google exactly what kind of business you are and what you offer.

02 — Answer Engine Optimization

Optimized for AI Search

Google's AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity — they all pull answers from content. AEO makes sure YOUR content gets pulled.

FAQ Sections

Clear questions and answers that AI can extract and cite directly.

Structured Data

FAQPage schema tells AI exactly which Q&As to pull from your page.

Natural Language

Content written the way people ask questions, not stuffed with keywords.
